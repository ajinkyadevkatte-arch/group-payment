Common Queries

I paid but didn't receive Telegram link

Don't worry — share your Razorpay Payment ID with us via Telegram or email. We'll verify the payment and grant access manually (usually within a few hours).

Payment debited but marked "Failed"

This is rare but happens occasionally. The amount is automatically reversed by your bank within 5–7 business days. No action needed from your side. If not credited after 10 days, contact us.

Want to upgrade or change plan?

Currently we don't offer plan upgrades mid-subscription. You can purchase the new plan separately at the end of your current cycle.

Concerned about content quality

Please refer to our Refund Policy — content satisfaction is not a refund ground, but we welcome your feedback to improve the service.
