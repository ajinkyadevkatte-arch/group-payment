Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2026 · Last Updated: 19 May 2026

⚠️ NO REFUND POLICY: All subscription purchases at AlphX Trading are final and non-refundable once Telegram channel access has been granted. Please read this policy carefully before subscribing.

1. No Refunds After Access Granted

Due to the nature of our digital subscription service, where access to private Telegram channels is delivered instantly upon successful payment, we maintain a strict no-refund policy. Once you receive the Telegram invite link, the subscription is deemed delivered and consumed.

This policy applies to all subscriptions, including:

  • Alpha Swing & Positional (1 Month / 3 Months / 1 Year)
  • AlphX Premium F&O (1 Month / 3 Months / 1 Year)
  • Ultimate Combo (Equity + F&O)
  • AlphX Discussion Group

2. Why No Refunds?

  • Instant delivery: Telegram access is granted within seconds of payment — there is no waiting period or trial.
  • Digital content: Once exposed to our analysis, calls, and strategies, the information cannot be "returned".
  • Educational service: This is an informational/educational membership, not a guarantee of trading profits.
  • Pre-payment disclosure: You are required to read and accept the disclaimer before subscribing.

3. Failed or Unsuccessful Payments

If your payment is debited from your bank but Razorpay reports a "failed" status, our system will not grant Telegram access. In such cases:

  • The amount will be automatically reversed by Razorpay/your bank within 5–7 business days.
  • No action is required from your side — the refund happens automatically.
  • If the amount is not credited back within 10 business days, please contact us with your payment ID.

4. Cancellation Before Telegram Access

If, for any technical reason, you completed a payment but did NOT receive the Telegram channel link (e.g., browser closed before success page loaded), please contact us within 24 hours with your Razorpay Payment ID. We will:

  • Verify the payment with Razorpay
  • Either grant you the Telegram access manually, OR
  • Process a full refund at our discretion if access cannot be granted

5. Service Disruption

If the Telegram channel is unilaterally discontinued by us before your subscription expires (excluding cases of policy violations on your part), we may, at our discretion, offer a pro-rata refund for the unused portion of your subscription.

6. No Refund Scenarios

Refunds will NOT be issued in the following cases:

  • You did not earn profits from the trade calls / signals
  • You incurred trading losses by following our content
  • You found the content unsuitable, too risky, or different from expectations
  • You did not actively use or read the channel content
  • You were removed from the channel for violating community rules (sharing content, harassment, etc.)
  • You did not have sufficient capital to follow the trades
  • You changed your mind after subscribing

7. Subscription Auto-Renewal

Currently, our subscriptions are one-time payments — they do NOT auto-renew. You will need to manually purchase a new subscription when your current one ends. This means there are no recurring charges to cancel.

8. Chargeback Policy

Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute through your bank without first contacting us is considered a violation of these terms and may result in:

  • Immediate termination of your Telegram access
  • Permanent ban from future AlphX Trading services
  • Legal action to recover the disputed amount and associated costs

Please always contact us first to resolve any payment-related concerns.

9. How to Contact Us About Refunds

For any genuine refund or payment-related concern (such as failed payment, technical issue, or service disruption), please reach out via our Contact Us page with:

  • Your full name as entered during checkout
  • Phone number used for subscription
  • Razorpay Payment ID (starts with pay_)
  • Brief description of the issue

We aim to respond within 48 business hours.

By subscribing to AlphX Trading, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to this Refund & Cancellation Policy. No refunds will be granted outside the specific scenarios listed above.
