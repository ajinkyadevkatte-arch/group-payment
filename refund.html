Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2026 · Last Updated: 19 May 2026

📋 Summary: AlphX Trading offers a 24-hour cooling-off period for buyer's remorse (subject to conditions below). After 24 hours or once significant channel content has been accessed, subscriptions become non-refundable due to the digital, instant-delivery nature of the service.

1. 24-Hour Cooling-Off Period

If you change your mind within 24 hours of subscription AND you have not accessed significant channel content (defined as: not joined the Telegram channel, OR joined but did not read more than 5 messages), you may request a full refund by contacting our support team.

To qualify for the cooling-off refund:

  • Request must be raised within 24 hours of payment timestamp
  • You should not have actively used the channel (verified via Telegram read receipts)
  • Submit request via Contact Us page with your Payment ID

2. No Refunds After 24 Hours or Active Use

After the 24-hour cooling-off period, or if you have actively accessed the channel content, subscriptions are final and non-refundable. This applies to all plans:

  • Alpha Swing & Positional (1 Month / 3 Months / 1 Year)
  • AlphX Premium F&O (1 Month / 3 Months / 1 Year)
  • Ultimate Combo (Equity + F&O)
  • AlphX Discussion Group

3. Why a Limited Refund Window?

  • Instant delivery: Telegram access is granted within seconds of payment — there is no waiting period or trial.
  • Digital content: Once exposed to our analysis and educational content, the information cannot be "returned".
  • Educational service: This is an informational/educational membership, not a guarantee of trading profits.
  • Pre-payment disclosure: You are required to read and accept the disclaimer before subscribing.

4. Failed or Unsuccessful Payments

If your payment is debited from your bank but the payment gateway reports a "failed" status, our system will not grant Telegram access. In such cases:

  • The amount will be automatically reversed by the payment gateway / your bank within 5–7 business days.
  • No action is required from your side — the refund happens automatically.
  • If the amount is not credited back within 10 business days, please contact us with your payment reference.

5. Cancellation Before Telegram Access

If, for any technical reason, you completed a payment but did NOT receive the Telegram channel link (e.g., browser closed before success page loaded), please contact us within 24 hours with your Payment ID. We will:

  • Verify the payment with the payment gateway
  • Either grant you the Telegram access manually, OR
  • Process a full refund at our discretion if access cannot be granted

6. Service Disruption

If the Telegram channel is unilaterally discontinued by us before your subscription expires (excluding cases of policy violations on your part), we will offer a pro-rata refund for the unused portion of your subscription.

7. Refund Processing Time

When a refund is approved (under section 1, 4, 5, or 6 above), the refund will be initiated within 2 business days via the original payment method. The amount typically reflects in your account within 5–7 business days depending on your bank.

8. No Refund Scenarios

Refunds will NOT be issued in the following cases:

  • You did not earn profits from the educational content / market analysis shared
  • You incurred trading losses by following our content
  • You found the content unsuitable, too risky, or different from expectations
  • You did not actively use or read the channel content (after 24-hour window)
  • You were removed from the channel for violating community rules
  • You did not have sufficient capital to follow the trade ideas discussed
  • Request made after 24 hours from purchase

9. Subscription Auto-Renewal

Currently, our subscriptions are one-time payments — they do NOT auto-renew. You will need to manually purchase a new subscription when your current one ends. This means there are no recurring charges to cancel.

10. Chargeback Policy

Initiating a chargeback or payment dispute through your bank without first contacting us is considered a violation of these terms and may result in:

  • Immediate termination of your Telegram access
  • Permanent ban from future AlphX Trading services
  • Legal action to recover the disputed amount and associated costs

Please always contact us first to resolve any payment-related concerns — we are committed to resolving genuine issues fairly.

11. How to Contact Us About Refunds

For any genuine refund or payment-related concern (cooling-off request, failed payment, technical issue, or service disruption), please reach out via our Contact Us page with:

  • Your full name as entered during checkout
  • Phone number used for subscription
  • Payment ID (provided on the success/receipt page)
  • Brief description of the issue

We aim to respond within 24 business hours.

By subscribing to AlphX Trading, you acknowledge that you have read and agreed to this Refund & Cancellation Policy. Refunds will be granted in the scenarios listed above; outside these specific scenarios, subscriptions are non-refundable due to the digital, instant-delivery nature of the service.
