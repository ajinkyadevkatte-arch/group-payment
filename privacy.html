Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 January 2026 · Last Updated: 19 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AlphX Trading ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit group-payment.vercel.app and subscribe to our paid services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information needed to deliver our subscription service:

  • Name — to personalize your subscription and receipt
  • Phone number — to contact you in case of payment or service issues
  • Payment details — handled directly by Razorpay; we receive only the payment ID after success
  • Subscription metadata — plan selected, duration, amount, payment status, and date

We do not collect email addresses, addresses, PAN, Aadhaar, bank details, or any other sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your subscription and grant Telegram channel access
  • To provide customer support and respond to your queries
  • To send you payment confirmations and service-related updates
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity

3. How We Store Your Information

Your subscription data is stored securely with our database provider Supabase, which uses industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Payment information is stored exclusively by Razorpay and is never visible to us. We do not store any credit/debit card or UPI details on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services We Use

  • Razorpay — payment processing (governed by Razorpay's Privacy Policy)
  • Supabase — secure database for subscription records
  • Telegram — content delivery (governed by Telegram's privacy policy)
  • Vercel — website hosting and serverless functions

5. We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or any other purposes. Your data is used solely to provide and improve the service you subscribed to.

6. Data Sharing

We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With payment processors (Razorpay) to complete transactions
  • When required by law, court order, or government request
  • To investigate, prevent, or take action regarding suspected fraud

7. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses minimal essential cookies necessary for the payment flow to function. We do not use third-party advertising or behavioural tracking cookies. We may use Vercel's standard server logs for performance monitoring and security purposes.

8. Data Retention

We retain your subscription records for as long as your subscription is active and for a reasonable period afterward (typically up to 7 years) for tax, accounting, and legal compliance purposes. You may request earlier deletion subject to legal retention requirements.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent and discontinue the service at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us via the Contact Us page.

10. Security

We follow reasonable security practices to protect your information, including HTTPS encryption, server-side signature verification of payments using HMAC-SHA256, and storing all secrets in encrypted environment variables. However, no online service can be 100% secure, and you use the service at your own risk.

11. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has shared information with us, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please visit our Contact Us page.

By using AlphX Trading, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.
